About Our Calculator

Understanding how we help government employees plan their retirement with accurate salary projections

Purpose of the Calculator

Our Government Employee Retirement Salary Calculator is designed to help central government employees in India project their retirement salary across multiple pay commissions. This tool provides accurate estimates by incorporating the complex rules and transitions between different pay commissions, from the 7th CPC through the upcoming 11th CPC.

The calculator addresses a critical need for government employees to understand how their salary will evolve over time, especially with the implementation of new pay commissions every 10 years. By providing detailed year-by-year projections, it helps employees make informed decisions about their financial planning and retirement preparation.

Whether you're planning for retirement in the next few years or just starting your government service career, this calculator offers valuable insights into your future earning potential and helps you understand the impact of various factors on your salary progression.

How It Works

Calculation Methodology

1
Annual Increments

Basic pay increases by 3% annually as per government rules

2
DA Accumulation

Dearness Allowance grows at user-specified rate (default 3.5%)

3
Pay Commission Transitions

Fitment factor applied to basic pay only, DA resets to 0%

4
HRA Calculation

House Rent Allowance calculated on current basic pay

Calculation Rules

Fitment Factor Application

When a new pay commission is implemented, the fitment factor is applied ONLY to the basic pay. The existing DA is not merged into basic pay for fitment calculation.

DA Reset Mechanism

After each pay commission transition, DA percentage resets to 0% and starts accumulating again from the new basic pay structure.
