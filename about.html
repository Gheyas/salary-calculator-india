About PayCommissions.in

India's most comprehensive retirement salary projection calculator for government employees, designed to help you plan your financial future with accurate pay commission transitions and DA calculations.

Purpose of Our Retirement Salary Calculator

Our Government Employee Retirement Salary Calculator is designed to help central government employees in India project their retirement salary across multiple pay commissions. This advanced pay commission calculator provides accurate estimates by incorporating the complex rules and transitions between different pay commissions, from the 7th CPC through the upcoming 11th CPC.

The calculator addresses a critical need for government employees to understand how their salary will evolve over time, especially with the implementation of new pay commissions every 10 years. Our retirement salary projection tool helps employees make informed decisions about their financial planning and retirement preparation.

Important Calculation Rules

Fitment Factor Application

When a new pay commission is implemented, the fitment factor is applied ONLY to the basic pay. The existing DA is not merged into basic pay for fitment calculation. This is crucial for accurate retirement salary projection calculations.

DA Reset Mechanism

After each pay commission transition, DA percentage resets to 0% and starts accumulating again from the new basic pay structure.
