1. Introduction

Welcome to PayCommissions.in ("we," "our," or "the Website"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

PayCommissions.in is an independent informational tool designed to help Indian government employees estimate their retirement salary. We are not affiliated with the Government of India or any official government agency.

2. Information We Collect

a. Salary Calculation Data

When you use our salary calculator, you voluntarily provide inputs such as your basic pay, DA percentage, and retirement year.

Important: All calculations are performed locally in your web browser. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal salary details on our servers.

b. Non-Personal Information

We may automatically collect non-personal information through:

  • Cookies: To enhance user experience and analyze site usage.
  • Server Logs: Standard logs including IP address, browser type, and pages visited for security and operational purposes.

3. Advertising and Cookies (AdSense Compliance)

To support our website, we display advertisements served by third-party vendors, including Google AdSense.

a. How Advertising Works

  • Third-Party Vendors: Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites.
  • Google's Advertising Cookies: Google's use of advertising cookies (such as the DoubleClick cookie) enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Data Sharing: Google and its advertising partners may share data collected through cookies with other ad technology providers to deliver personalized advertising experiences across the web.

b. Consent Management for EEA/UK Users

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and Switzerland: We use a Google-certified Consent Management Platform (CMP) to obtain your explicit consent for personalized advertising before any advertising cookies are placed on your device. You can manage your consent preferences through the cookie banner that appears when you first visit our site.

c. Your Advertising Choices

You have full control over personalized advertising:

  • Opt Out of Google Ads: Visit Google's Ad Settings to opt out of personalized advertising.
  • Industry-Wide Opt-Out: Visit www.aboutads.info/choices/ to opt out of third-party vendors' personalized advertising.
  • Additional Ad Networks: Other third-party vendors or ad networks may also serve ads on this site if you have not opted out.

4. How We Use Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the website.
  • To personalize your experience and improve our services.
  • To understand and analyze how you use our website.
  • To display relevant advertisements to support the site.
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to legal requests.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your salary calculation inputs are stored only on your device using browser local storage. This data is not accessible to us and never leaves your browser. While we do not store your personal salary data on our servers, we implement industry-standard security measures, including:

  • HTTPS/SSL Encryption: All connections to our website are encrypted.
  • Secure Hosting: Our website is hosted on secure infrastructure.
  • Regular Security Updates: We maintain up-to-date security protocols.

6. Your Data Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Clear Your Data: You can clear any data stored in your browser's local storage by clearing your browser's cache and site data.
  • Control Cookies: You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or our consent management platform.
  • Withdraw Consent: For EEA/UK users, you can withdraw your consent for advertising cookies at any time through the consent banner or cookie settings.
  • Do Not Track: We respect "Do Not Track" signals. If you have DNT enabled, we will limit data collection from your session where technically feasible.
  • Access and Deletion: Since we don't store personal data on our servers, there is no data to access or delete from our systems.

7. Analytics Services

We may use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics. This service:

  • Collects aggregated, anonymized data about site visits and user behavior
  • Does not track personally identifiable information
  • Helps us improve website functionality and user experience

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

8. Calculator Disclaimer

Important: All salary projections provided by our calculator are estimates for planning purposes only and do not guarantee specific outcomes. Actual retirement benefits depend on official government policies, amendments, and individual service records. For official retirement benefit information, please consult the Department of Pension & Pensioners' Welfare or your department's administrative office.

9. Legal Compliance

Our data practices are designed to comply with applicable laws, including:

  • India: Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), 2023
  • European Union: General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Advertising Policies: Google AdSense Program Policies and EU User Consent Policy

As we primarily process calculation data on the client-side (in your browser), our focus is on ensuring transparency and respecting your privacy choices.

10. External Links

Our website contains links to official government resources for your reference. These external websites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content or data practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our data practices, new features, or legal requirements. We will notify users of material changes by displaying a notice on our homepage for 30 days. Continued use of the website after such changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

Email: privacy@paycommissions.in

Website: https://paycommissions.in

Response Time: We aim to respond within 7 business days.

13. Consent

By using PayCommissions.in, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please discontinue use of the website.
