1. Introduction

Welcome to PayCommissions.in ("we," "our," or "the Website"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage when you use our Government Employee Retirement Salary Calculator.

PayCommissions.in is an independent informational tool designed to help Indian government employees estimate their retirement salary based on Central Pay Commission structures and historical patterns. We are not affiliated with the Government of India or any official government agency.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you use our salary calculator, you voluntarily provide the following information:

  • Date of Birth: To calculate years until retirement
  • Date of Joining Service: To determine years of service
  • Current Pay Level and Stage: To establish your current position in the pay matrix
  • Current Basic Pay: For salary calculations
  • Dearness Allowance (DA): Current DA percentage
  • HRA City Classification: To calculate housing allowance
  • Retirement Age: Expected retirement age (58, 60, or 62 years)

Important: All calculations are performed locally in your web browser. We do not transmit your salary details to our servers or any third party.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may collect non-personal information automatically through:

  • Browser Type and Version: For compatibility optimization
  • Device Information: Operating system, screen resolution
  • IP Address: For analytics and security (anonymized)
  • Page Views and Navigation Patterns: To improve user experience
  • Time Spent on Pages: For analytics purposes

2.3 Legal Obligations

We may process information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal requests from government authorities.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • Calculation Services: To perform retirement salary projections
  • Website Improvement: To enhance user experience and functionality
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and popular features
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Communication: To respond to inquiries (only if you contact us)
  • Legal Compliance: To meet regulatory requirements

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Client-Side Storage

Your salary calculation inputs are stored only on your device using browser local storage technology. This means:

  • Data never leaves your computer or phone
  • We cannot access your calculations
  • You can clear this data anytime via browser settings
  • Data persists only on the specific device and browser you use

4.2 Security Measures

While we do not collect or store your personal data on our servers, we implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All connections are secured
  • Regular Security Audits: To identify vulnerabilities
  • Secure Hosting: Using reputable cloud service providers
  • Access Controls: Limited administrative access

4.3 Data Retention

Since calculations are performed client-side, your inputs remain on your device until you clear browser data. No retention period applies to server-stored data as we don't store any personal information on our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 External Links

Our website contains links to official government resources for your reference:

  • Department of Expenditure (Government of India)
  • Central Pay Commission reports
  • Pension and gratuity guidelines

These external websites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content or data practices.

5.2 Analytics Services

We may use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics. This service:

  • Does not collect personally identifiable information
  • Uses cookies to track aggregate user behavior
  • Helps us improve website performance and content

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we may hold (though we store minimal data)
  • Correction: Update any inaccurate information (via calculator inputs)
  • Deletion: Clear browser local storage to remove all calculation data
  • Portability: Export your calculations (feature available in calculator)
  • Objection: Opt out of analytics tracking

6.2 Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block All Cookies: Disable cookies entirely (may affect functionality)
  • Delete Cookies: Remove existing cookies from your device
  • Third-Party Cookies: Selectively block external tracking cookies

6.3 Do Not Track

We respect "Do Not Track" browser signals. If enabled, we will not collect analytics data from your session.

7. Children's Privacy

PayCommissions.in is designed for adult government employees and job candidates. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18 years of age. If you believe a minor has provided information to us, please contact us immediately.

8. Calculator Accuracy and Disclaimers

8.1 Calculation Methodology

Our salary projections are based on:

  • 7th Central Pay Commission pay matrix structure
  • Historical DA increment patterns (3% per year average)
  • Standard annual increment progression (one stage per year)
  • Official HRA percentages by city classification
  • Publicly available government circulars

8.2 Limitations and Disclaimers

Disclaimer: All projections are estimates for planning purposes only. Actual retirement salary depends on:

  • Future government pay commission decisions
  • DA revision policies (can be modified)
  • Promotion schedules (not predictable)
  • Service rule changes
  • Department-specific allowances

8.3 Not Official Advice

This calculator does not guarantee specific salary outcomes. For official retirement benefit information, consult:

  • Your department's establishment section
  • Pension Disbursing Authority (PDA)
  • Central Pension Accounting Office (CPAO)
  • State government pension departments (for state employees)

11. Legal Compliance

Our processing of information complies with:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000 (India)
  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (when enacted)
  • International data protection standards where applicable

Since we process data locally on your device and do not store sensitive personal information on servers, we maintain minimal data protection obligations while respecting your privacy rights.

12. International Users

PayCommissions.in is primarily designed for Indian government employees. If you access our website from outside India:

  • Our calculators use Indian pay commission structures
  • Calculations may not be relevant for non-Indian government employees
  • Data processing occurs client-side (on your device)
  • Minimal data is transferred across borders
