1. Introduction

Welcome to PayCommissions.in ("we," "our," or "the Website"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

PayCommissions.in is an independent informational tool designed to help Indian government employees estimate their retirement salary. We are not affiliated with the Government of India or any official government agency.

2. Information We Collect

a. Salary Calculation Data

When you use our salary calculator, you voluntarily provide inputs such as your basic pay, DA percentage, and retirement year. Important: All calculations are performed locally in your web browser. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal salary details on our servers.

b. Non-Personal Information

We may automatically collect non-personal information through:

  • Cookies: To enhance user experience and analyze site usage.
  • Server Logs: Standard logs including IP address, browser type, and pages visited for security and operational purposes.

3. Advertising Privacy (AdSense Compliance)

To support our website, we display advertisements served by third-party vendors, including Google AdSense.

  • Third-Party Vendors: Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites.
  • Google's Advertising Cookies: Google's use of advertising cookies (such as the DoubleClick cookie) enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Opting Out: You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices/.

If you have not opted out of third-party ad serving, the cookies of other third-party vendors or ad networks may also be used to serve ads on this site.

4. How We Use Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the website.
  • To personalize your experience and improve our services.
  • To understand and analyze how you use our website.
  • To display relevant advertisements to support the site.
  • To comply with legal obligations and legal requests.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your salary calculation inputs are stored only on your device using browser local storage. This data is not accessible to us. While we do not store your personal data on our servers, we implement industry-standard security measures, including HTTPS (SSL), to protect our website.

6. Your Data Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Clear Your Data: You can clear any data stored in your browser's local storage by clearing your browser's cache and site data.
  • Control Cookies: You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.
  • Do Not Track: We respect "Do Not Track" signals. If you have DNT enabled, we will limit data collection from your session where technically feasible.

7. Disclaimer

All salary projections are estimates for planning purposes only and do not guarantee specific outcomes. For official retirement benefit information, please consult the Department of Pension & Pensioners' Welfare or your department's administrative office.

8. Legal Compliance

Our data practices are designed to comply with applicable laws, including India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), 2023. As we primarily process data on the client-side, our focus is on ensuring transparency and respecting your privacy choices.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify users of material changes by displaying a notice on our homepage. Continued use of the website after such changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
